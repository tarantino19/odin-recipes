Adobo Recipe

Home Page

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken parts, cut into serving portions
  • 2 Tbsp crushed garlic
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp peppercorn, crushed
  • 1/4 cup oil, for frying

How to Cook & Prepare

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over med-high heat. Add the chicken and drumsticks (skin side down) to the pan and brown on all sides. Remove and place on a plate. Drain off grease.
  3. To the skillet, add the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, half the chilies, sugar, black pepper, and bay leaves. Bring to a boil over med-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken, skin side up, reduce heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 30-35 minutes until cooked through and tender.
  5. Remove chicken from pan onto a clean plate. Bring sauce to a boil over med-high heat and cook uncovered for about 5 minutes or until slightly thickened. (Add cornstarch and water if a thicker sauce is desired.)
  6. Remove the pan from the heat, discard bay leaves, and add the chicken back. Spoon sauce over the top of the chicken and garnish with remaining chilies.
  7. Serve chicken and sauce over jasmine rice.
